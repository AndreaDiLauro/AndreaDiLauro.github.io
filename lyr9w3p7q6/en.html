About

Andrea Di Lauro trained in cinematic acting at the Roma Film Academy (2022–2025) and studies DAMS at the University of Teramo. She completed a linguistic high school and speaks multiple languages, bringing strong text work and adaptability to ensemble and lead roles.

Her on-camera credits include short films such as Cibele (2024), Girls (2023), and La fantasmagorica avventura di un cellulare (2023), as well as the music video Euforia (2023). Andrea complements her screen experience with stage and physical skills — stage combat, basic singing, horseback riding, swimming and team sports.

Andrea is based in Rome and is available for auditions in Italy and internationally. She brings linguistic flexibility (Italian, English, Spanish, German) and an approach grounded in collaboration and character truth.
